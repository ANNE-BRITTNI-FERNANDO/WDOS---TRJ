Outdoor Dining

Experience the charm of outdoor dining at our hotels. Enjoy al fresco meals in a delightful ambiance, surrounded by nature and fresh air. Discover exquisite cuisine and impeccable service while savoring the beauty of the great outdoors at our hotels.



Photography Workshops

Unlock your creativity through our photography workshops. Join us to explore the art and techniques of photography in a relaxed and inspiring setting. Learn from seasoned photographers, capture stunning moments, and refine your photography skills.

Bird Watching

Embark on a tranquil journey into the world of birds with our bird-watching experiences. Immerse yourself in the beauty of nature as you observe a diverse array of bird species in their natural habitats. Our guided bird-watching tours are led by knowledgeable experts who will help you spot and identify various birds, from colorful songbirds to majestic raptors.

Environmental Education Programs

Explore, learn, and connect with nature through our engaging environmental education programs. Designed for all ages, our programs provide hands-on experiences that deepen your understanding of the natural world and foster a sense of stewardship for the environment.

Campfires and Stargazing

Experience the magic of campfires and stargazing at our enchanting location. Our campfire and stargazing events provide the perfect opportunity to unwind, connect with nature, and enjoy the beauty of the night sky.


Forest Treasure Hunts

Embark on an exciting forest treasure hunt adventure! Our forest treasure hunts are a thrilling way to explore the beauty of the wilderness while engaging in a fun and interactive activity. Join us for a forest treasure hunt and create unforgettable memories as you explore the great outdoors in a whole new way!
